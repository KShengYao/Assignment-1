Mrs. GREEN APPLE - インフェルノ（Inferno）

02.08.2019

Description: Inferno (インフェルノ or Inferuno in Japanese) is a song released by the Japanese rock band Mrs. GREEN APPLE. It was released as a part of the group’s album, Attitude in 2019. It was used as the opening theme for Season 1 of the anime Fire Force (Enn Enn no Shouboutai or 炎炎ノ消防隊 in Japanese).
